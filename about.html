Hey, I'm Peggy

I care about making things easier for people. As a designer, I thrive at the intersection of human behaviour and technology – focused on understanding pain points, creating solutions, and crafting usable, impactful experiences.

I believe technology should be designed so that people can use it.

Product Designer at Sitemate

Design roles are evolving – and I'm here for it! I design and take on PM duties for Sitemate's core product Dashpivot, which helps industrial companies manage their data, stay compliant and streamline site operations. I own features from 0 to 1, talk to customers, solve problems, and provide strategic input to our roadmap. Sometimes it gets chaotic, but I love it!
